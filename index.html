Frequently Asked Questions

You can start a SIP with as little as ₹500 per month. For lump-sum investments, the minimum varies by fund — typically ₹1,000 to ₹5,000. There is no minimum for advisory services, though we recommend having at least ₹2 lakhs for a meaningful financial plan.

We operate on a transparent, fee-only model for advisory services. For mutual fund investments, we earn distributor commissions from fund houses — which are fully disclosed. We never charge hidden fees. A detailed fee schedule is shared before engagement.

Your investments are held directly in your name or in your demat account — we never hold client funds. We are SEBI registered, AMFI certified, and fully regulated. All mutual fund investments are with AMFI-registered AMCs. Your capital safety is our highest priority.

Yes, most mutual fund investments (open-ended funds) can be redeemed within 1-3 business days. ELSS funds have a 3-year lock-in. We advise staying invested for the recommended period to achieve your financial goals, but you retain complete control and liquidity.

Yes, we have a dedicated NRI investment desk. We assist with NRE/NRO account-based investments, FEMA compliance, repatriation advisory, and building India-focused portfolios. We work with NRIs across the US, UK, UAE, Singapore, and Australia.

All portfolios are reviewed quarterly as standard. For active clients, we do monthly check-ins and ad-hoc reviews whenever markets are significantly volatile or your personal circumstances change. Annual comprehensive financial plan reviews are included for all advisory clients.
